Why Build
With Us?

Climate Focus

Marble is the only deeptech venture studio in Europe focused exclusively on climate. Our north star is to erase at least one billion tons from annual emissions, about 2% of the current global carbon budget. We need many shots on goal to achieve this. This means we optimise for this outcome, and provide the best specialised support to Founders committed to this mission.

Team & Network

With expertise in applied science, climate, and a track record in company building, our core team will shortcut your learning and make sure you’re on the right track. Our Partners have supported hundreds of pre-company Founders, who went on to create 30+ startups in AI, biotech, materials, fusion, and more. As advisors or angel investors, we helped them raise capital from top VCs, for over €120m in value creation. At Marble, you be able will tap into an unparalleled network of technical experts, industry leaders, climate investors, mentors and emerging Founders.

Insights & Expertise

Finding a high leverage climate idea can be hard. Turning it into a successful company is even harder. At Marble we actively scope opportunity areas in climate deeptech. You get the support of an in-house research team, insights from our investment thesis, and streamlined access to our expert network. So you’re not starting from scratch.

Optimised Process

We bet on the combination of Founder potential and well-defined problems to develop radical solutions for hard climate problems. This differs from other programmes designed for IP commercialisation or large cohorts. We call it "reverse tech transfer.” Through systematic de-risking at every step of the way, we’ll help you build a venture optimised for climate impact and market success.

Funding

You’ll be supported with a €2,500 monthly payment through the programme. If you succeed, we will invest €250,000 in your new company. You and your team will own 80% at pre-seed. While other studios tend to keep a much larger share, our ratio incentivises everyone for long-term success, and leaves more room for follow-on investors.

Ecosystem

We are based in Paris, France, with access to top talent, science, capital, and corporate customers, local and international. Working in the heart of Europe, you will leverage an ecosystem with strong incentives toward net zero targets, and high arbitrage opportunity for ambitious deeptech Founders. We can sponsor your Founder Visa if you wish to relocate in France. Remote participation is possible, preferably from Europe and with regular in-person meetings.

Find The Best Idea

Timeline 3 months
Funding €2,500 per month

During Explore, we will scope opportunity areas where your skills can unlock radical solutions to hard climate problems. Together, we will generate and evaluate multiple venture hypotheses using a range of methods like structured ideation, technical landscaping, market research, expert interviews, and techno-economic analysis. We then refine hypotheses until we land on a breakthrough idea with massive climate impact and business potential.

At the end of this phase, we’ll review the work we’ve done and decide whether we progress into Create and turn it into a company.

Turn The Idea Into a New Company

Timeline 6 months
Funding €2,500 per month

During Create, we will work together to turn the most promising idea into an investable pre-seed company. Our main goals will be to validate the technical strategy and go-to-market, demonstrate customer traction, define a clear roadmap with key milestones, and most importantly, source top co-founders and advisors. In some cases an early prototype could even exist at this stage. At the end of this phase, you and your co-founders will pitch in front of our investment committee.

Who Should
Apply?

We are looking for scientists, engineers and operators with deep domain expertise related to opportunity areas we’re pursuing. We are also open to hear about broader idea spaces in climate on which we haven’t formed a thesis yet. Our two most important selection criteria are your technical/domain insights as well as your potential to lead a massively scalable company. No need for a team, IP, or even a specific idea.
